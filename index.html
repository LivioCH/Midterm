INTERESTS

Playing Games

I’ve always enjoyed gaming, starting with Minecraft when I was young, where I’d spend hours building and exploring with friends. By seventh grade, I got into Mobile Legends(A MOBA Game), a popular game in Indonesia, which became a favorite I still play with friends today. During the COVID pandemic, I discovered Genshin Impact, an RPG with a rich world that was a refreshing change. Playing these three games has become a way to connect with friends and immerse myself in different worlds, though sometimes I enjoy taking breaks, especially from the more intense games like Mobile Legends.

Listening to Music

I really like hearing different type of music,i’m drawn to artists from different places each bringing something unique to my playlist. TULUS, an Indonesian solo artist, has a way of connecting deeply with emotions, his songs often moving me. Then there’s DAY6, a Korean band that deserves more recognition,they create incredible music that’s still relatively underrated. ERIC CHOU, a popular Taiwanese singer, sings with such emotion that when I translate the lyrics, the impact of his music becomes even stronger. Lastly, LADY GAGA, an American icon, her powerful voice never fails to energize me.
